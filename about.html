Our Story

Built for little riders.

TinyTrail Bikes is a premium children’s bike brand built to inspire first rides, outdoor confidence, and healthy family habits. Our designs prioritize safety controls, lightweight materials, and intuitive sizes that kids love.

Whether you're teaching balance on a 12-inch training bike, cruising the neighborhood on a 16-inch frame, or embarking on longer trail rides with our sporty 20-inch adventure models, TinyTrail provides the support families need.
